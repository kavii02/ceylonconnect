Who We Are?

The team behind CeylonConnect are a passionate group of innovators committed to creating a platform where individuals can share skills, knowledge and every day things, without having to use conventional currency. We believe that everyone has something valuable to contribute from all walks of life, to promote Kindness, Learning and Collaboration. We built our platform out of desire to foster community connection, sustainability, safe, and rewarding.

What makes us flexible, we are a movement toward collaboration, skill building, caring for each other of sharing and cutting on plastic.

Our App

CeylonConnect works by allowing users to create a profile where they can list the skills or goods they are willing to offer and specify what they are looking for in return. Users can then browse or search through other members‚Äô offers using keywords and filters to find a suitable match. Once they find an offer that meets their needs, they can send a request to the other user, negotiate the terms of the exchange, and agree on a time and place either online or in person to complete the exchange. Afterward, both parties can leave reviews about the experience, helping build trust and reputation within the community. Throughout the process, no money is involved; instead, it‚Äôs all about sharing, learning, and supporting each other
